What does this mean for you?

My psychological ownership of what I commit myself to makes me a valuable partner when quality is on the line.

DEVELOPER:

I recognize and cultivate potential in others. I spot the signs of small improvement and derive satisfaction from evidence of progress.

What makes me valuable?

Encouraging others comes natural to me. I can see raw potential in people and actively invest in their development by encouraging them to learn, grow and improve.

What does this mean for you?

I am genuinely interested in other people's progress. I can pin point improvement opportunities they may not see for themselves.

ACHIEVER:

I have a great deal of stamina, and I work hard. I take great satisfaction from being busy and productive.

What makes me valuable?

I love to complete tasks, and my accomplishments fulfill me. I have a strong inner drive - an innate source of intensity, energy and power that motivate me to work hard to get things done.

What does this mean for you?

I have an inner drive that enables me to persevere until the goal is reached. I will read, listen, observe, discuss, experiment and experience as much as I can to fill my mind with fresh ideas.

BELIEF:

I have certain core values that are unchanging. Out of these values emerges a defined purpose for my life.

What makes me valuable?

My core values guide me and give me purpose. I provide clarity, conviction and stability by living my values. By nature, I have clearly definied principles that set the tone for my life and influence my behavior. I take pride and joy in doing the right thing, even when it's hard.

What does this mean for you?

I am trustworthy. You can depend on me, not only to get the job done, but to get the job done right.

POSITIVITY:

I have contagious enthusiasm. I am upbeat and can get others excited about the task at hand.

What does this mean for you?
